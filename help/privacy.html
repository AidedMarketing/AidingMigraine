Privacy First, Always

Aiding Migraine was built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. Your migraine data stays on YOUR device, period.

The Short Version

We know nobody reads long privacy policies, so here's the essential summary:

  • ‚úÖ Your migraine data never leaves your device
  • ‚úÖ We don't collect personal health information
  • ‚úÖ We don't sell, share, or monetize your data
  • ‚úÖ No account required, no login, no tracking
  • ‚úÖ Works completely offline
  • ‚úÖ You can delete everything anytime

That's it. If you want more details, keep reading.

What We Collect

üîî Notification Preferences (Minimal Server Data)

If you enable notifications, we store:

  • Push subscription token: Anonymous identifier needed to send notifications
  • Notification time: Your preferred time for daily check-ins (e.g., "8:00 PM")
  • Timezone: Your timezone (e.g., "America/New_York") to deliver notifications at the correct local time
  • Enabled status: Whether notifications are on or off

Important: This data is stored on our notification server and is necessary for the notification system to function. However:

  • The subscription token is generated by your browser/device, not by us
  • It's anonymous - not linked to your name, email, or identity
  • It cannot be used to identify you personally
  • You can delete it anytime by disabling notifications

üíæ Migraine Data (Local Storage Only)

All your migraine tracking data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Migraine entries (dates, times, severity, duration)
  • Calendar data
  • Analytics calculations
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device. It's stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to our servers, cloud services, or third parties.

What We Don't Collect

Just as important as what we collect is what we don't collect:

  • ‚ùå No personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • ‚ùå No migraine symptoms or health data (we never see your entries)
  • ‚ùå No location data (beyond timezone for notification delivery)
  • ‚ùå No analytics or usage tracking (we don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • ‚ùå No advertising IDs or tracking cookies
  • ‚ùå No browsing history or behavioral data
  • ‚ùå No device fingerprinting

Your Rights

‚úÖ Right to Access

Your data is stored on your device, accessible anytime. You can view all your migraine entries in the calendar and analytics pages.

‚úÖ Right to Export

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Export PDF Report" to generate a comprehensive report of your migraine history. This is your data - export it whenever you want.

‚úÖ Right to Delete

Delete your data anytime:

  • Individual entries: Tap any date on the calendar and delete the entry
  • All data: Settings ‚Üí "Clear All Data" (permanent - cannot be undone)
  • Notification data: Disable notifications in Settings to remove server-side data

‚úÖ Right to Opt-Out

Don't want notifications? Simply don't enable them, or disable them in Settings at any time. The app works perfectly fine without notifications.

‚úÖ No Account Needed

We don't require account creation, so there's no profile to manage or account to delete. Just use the app.

Data Storage

üì± Local Storage (On Your Device)

How long: Until you delete it or clear your browser data
Who has access: Only you (via your device)

üîî Notification Server (Our Servers)

We do NOT use:

  • Social media pixels (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers or third-party data sharing

Security

We take security seriously:

  • HTTPS encryption: All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted
  • No passwords: No account = no password to leak
  • Local storage: Your migraine data never travels over the network, so it can't be intercepted
  • Open source: Our code is publicly available for security review

Your responsibility:

  • Keep your device locked with a passcode/biometric
  • Don't share your device with untrusted people
  • Keep your operating system and browser updated

Children's Privacy

Aiding Migraine is designed for adults with migraines. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has used Aiding Migraine, please contact us.

However, since we don't collect personal identifying information and data is stored locally, there's minimal privacy risk even if a minor uses the app with parental consent.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data. Ever.

The only "sharing" that happens is when YOU choose to export a PDF and share it with your doctor - which is entirely under your control.

We will never:

  • Sell your data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties
  • Share your migraine information with insurance companies
  • Provide your data to researchers without explicit consent
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

Legal Requirements

Because we don't collect personal health information, there's essentially nothing to provide even if legally compelled. The only data on our servers is anonymous notification preferences, which cannot identify you.

International Users

Aiding Migraine can be used anywhere in the world. Since migraine data is stored locally on your device:

  • No international data transfer of health information
  • Compliant with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other privacy regulations
  • Notification server hosted in the United States (via Render.com)

If you're in the EU: You have all GDPR rights (access, deletion, portability) as described in the "Your Rights" section above.
