What This Shows

Example Attack: Started Nov 26 at 9:31am, Duration: 47 hours (through Nov 27-28)
  • Left Calendar (Current): Only Nov 26 is colored, no indication attack continued
  • Right Calendar (New): Nov 26 fully colored, Nov 27-28 show faded color (35% opacity)
  • Benefits: You can still easily count distinct attacks (count solid squares), but now see total days impacted
  • Clean Design: The fade creates a natural "ongoing" feeling without overwhelming the calendar
  • Quick Scanning: At a glance you see "1 attack starting, 3 total days affected"
