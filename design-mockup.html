Summary of Changes

  • Remove all emojis and replace with minimal SVG icons + text labels
  • Simplify Log page with dropdown pain scale and collapsible sections
  • Reduce button count by ~40% through progressive disclosure
  • Offer 6 theme options: Warm Dark, Cool Dark, Warm Light, Cool Light, High Contrast, Current
  • Improve accessibility with proper ARIA labels and semantic HTML
  • Increase white space throughout for reduced cognitive load
  • Consistent icon library (Feather-style) across entire app
  • Larger font sizes (16-18px minimum) for easier reading
  • Better information hierarchy in history cards and analytics
  • No pure white/black - all colors muted for eye comfort
